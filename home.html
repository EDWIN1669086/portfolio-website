My Community

Hispanic Scholarship Fund (HSF)

Being an HSF Scholar since my junior year in high school has allowed me to call HSF a home away from home. I grew up in an Asian conclave known as Little Saigon, where less than 10% of students are Hispanics, so having this representation keeps me motivated and passionate to see Latinx success. Thus in 2022, I became a year-long mentor to 5 high school seniors, teaching them about the college application process, scholarships, and leadership.

Orange County United Way (OCUW)

OCUW was my first introduction to the professional workforce. During their summer-long virtual employability academy in 2020, I learned how to create my own website, design my own business card, use Excel, and tactfully answer interview questions. Now, I join their organization as an alumni, giving back to my Orange County community by preparing high school students for college.
